Who We Are

Hello, our names are Lily Su, Kiki Wang, and Audrey Zhou, and we are a group of first-year students. We are all beginner programmers, who came together to create a project for 2022 Pearl Hacks. This is our first hackathon, so we have been able to learn many things from the workshops, and from coding together as a team. We hope you enjoy our demo.

How It Works

  • Using our beginner's knowledge of programming, we have hard-coded a website, where users can input a word to generate a piece of art. The art is created by a Python library called turtle, and our art pieces consists of different shapes that can be made through turtle.

Make Your Own!

Use our random Python Turtle art generator to create a masterpiece. Enter in a word with no special characters, spaces, or capital letters for the best result.
